About Me

Hey there! I'm Meera, an undergrad in computer science. I’m proficient in Python, Java, and C++, but I still struggle to speak human before my morning coffee. My passion for technology and problem-solving often leads to wild adventures in debugging and the occasional victory dance when my code finally works.

I've dabbled in various projects, from web development to data analysis. When I’m not glued to my screen, I’m probably reading, cooking, working out, dressing up, or binging Studio Ghibli movies, which keeps me somewhat sane and well-rounded.

I’m particularly interested in machine learning, web development, and data security, and I dream of one day building something that will make people say, “Wow, that’s cool!” or at least, “Hey, it works!”
